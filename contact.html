Frequently Asked Questions

Is this website free?

Yes! Fit With Abhi is completely free. All tools, calculators, and content are available without any cost or sign-up.

Is the meal plan suitable for everyone?

The meal plans shown are Abhi's personal plans tailored to his goals (21M, 180cm, 81kg). Use the Calorie Calculator to find your personalized targets.

How accurate is the AI scanner?

The scanner uses TensorFlow.js MobileNet and identifies general object categories. It works well for common foods and gym equipment but may not identify specific brands. Everything runs in your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

Is my data stored somewhere?

All data (hydration logs, etc.) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Clearing browser data will reset everything.
